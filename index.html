About me

Hi, I’m Ridwan Ahmed Piyas

I graduated with MBBS from Shaheed Monsur Ali Medical College, Uttara , and have dedicated my career to internal medicine and preventive healthcare. Over the last decade I’ve worked in both urban hospitals and rural outreach programs — because I believe quality healthcare is a right.

My approach is simple: listen thoroughly, explain clearly, and involve you in every decision. I’m also a visiting faculty at two medical colleges, because teaching keeps me sharp.

The good physician treats the disease; the great physician treats the patient who has the disease.
