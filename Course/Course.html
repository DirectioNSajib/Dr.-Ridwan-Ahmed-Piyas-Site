User-Centered Design in Healthcare

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Professor of Clinical Medicine • 15+ years experience

Definition

An iterative medical approach that focuses on patients and their families, emphasizing the importance of creating intuitive, accessible, and compassionate healthcare experiences.

Page 1/15

Key Principles:

  • Patient-first approach in all decisions
  • Iterative feedback from patients and families
  • Accessible communication and clear explanations
  • Empathy and understanding in every interaction
